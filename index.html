Where the numbers come from
This tool uses publicly available Canadian federal and provincial datasets to generate estimates. All sources are cited below. Calculations are benchmarks, not site-specific energy audits.
⚠️
This is a screening tool, not an energy audit. Estimates are based on published Canadian averages and benchmarks. Actual savings and costs vary significantly by building age, condition, local contractor rates, and occupancy patterns. Results should be validated by a certified energy advisor (NRCan EnerGuide) before making investment decisions.
